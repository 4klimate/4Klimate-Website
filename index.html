Klimate is a website designed for AngelHacks 2.0
that gives you helpful suggestions on how to help
the environment! Klimate was created to help people
help the environment without having to spend a lot
of money. To get started, head to the sign up page and
create an account to get redirected to your profile page
where you can see your stats and daily suggestion!
Make sure to check the leaderboard to see the
users who have completed the most challenges!
